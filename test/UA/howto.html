How to play Universal Attractor

Generators

Generators would generates either stars or lower tiered ones. Stars is a main currency that is produced by generators which you can buy more generators.

As the bonus, you can get a x1.05 production upgrade by buying it.

Prestige

When you feel like hitting a wall, you can reset a game but you get a boost for all production called prestige power. Prestige requires e38 stars to reset and you can not get less prestige power by resetting anytime.

Transfer

There is another layer of prestige which includes to resets your prestige stuff than normal prestige. Other than prestige, you can access to upgrades but everything has a cost of different currency called transfer points.

You can get your first transfer point by reaching 100x prestige power and you can get more by reaching higher prestige powers.

Supernova

In Javascript, there is the upper limit of all normal numbers is 1.79769313e308, which is called Infinity. But however, when you reach Infinity stars, the star amount will cause to overflow and force you to reset again, including transfer points and upgrades.

Every single supernova will give you 1 neutron stars, which you can spend on supernova content.

Supernova Upgrades

Supernova Upgrades is the first content of Supernova. These upgrades will boost your progression, including generators, prestige, and transfer content.

Achievements

There is another way to boost your things. You can get it by reaching a goal but you need to supernova too. The rewards of this pack is related to the achievement.

Autobuyers

Autobuyers is the second content of supernova. After reaching Infinity, you will able to automate the last part before reaching it.

There is a interval waiting for your autobuyers to take action. You can reduce the interval by 20% down to 50 milliseconds by buying with neutron stars.

Challenges

Challenges is the third content of supernova. Challenges are special progressions that you have to start over and have to reach Infinity with special conditions. But some supernova upgrades are disabled in all challenges because it's powerful that you can complete challenges easily.

After completing for first time in any challenge, you will reward reward boxes, which you can open it but it takes time to get more autobuyers in back-to-back progression before supernova.

Buyinshop

Buyinshop is the fourth content of supernova. It's a shop that involves shop features for autobuyers.

Some buyinshop features are expensive that you recommend to break limit! After reaching million neutron stars, you notice you can supernova automatically in no time!

Neutron boosts

Neutron boosts is the fifth content of supernova. Beside neutron boosts, you can able to break the limit of 1.8e308! If you have more stars above 1.8e308, you will able to gain more neutron stars in smaller amounts of supernovas.

There is infinitely repeating upgrades that speed up the production of the generators. There is also the upgrade that increases the PP gain too.

Neutron tiers

Neutron tiers is the second content after breaking the limit. Like the set of generators, all neutron tiers are unlocked but have the cost of neutron stars and higher bonuses for buying it.

First neutron tier will produce neutrons, which reduces the cost of the normal generators.
